Technology skills get you the interview; your mindset and problem-solving approach get you the job. This section focuses on developing the mental models and productive habits of elite engineers, amplified by AI.

The First-Principles Engineer

Great engineers don't just know *how* to use a tool; they understand *why* it works. They break down complex problems into their fundamental truths. Before starting any task, ask:

  • What is the absolute most fundamental problem I am trying to solve?
  • What are the core constraints and requirements? (e.g., latency, consistency, cost)
  • What are the knowns and unknowns?
  • How would I solve this if I couldn't use any existing frameworks?
